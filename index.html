About Akela Poultry

Established in 2009, we have grown into a trusted partner for poultry farmers across the region, dedicated to empowering your success in chicken farming. From our beginnings as a reliable supplier of high-quality chicks, premium feed, and essential medicines, we've expanded our commitment to deliver comprehensive support that ensures healthy flocks and thriving businesses. What sets us apart is our hands-on approach. Our experienced supervisors and veterinarians conduct regular farm visits, providing expert guidance on best practices—from optimal feeding strategies and disease prevention to efficient rearing techniques. Over the years, we've helped countless farmers boost productivity, reduce losses, and achieve sustainable profits. Our mission remains simple: to foster a stronger poultry industry through quality products, personalized service, and unwavering reliability.
